Implementing Models

To date we have focused on core computational skills — root finding, integration, curve fitting; ode, etc — now we want to use those kills to study some models. Last week we looked at the damped pendulum. This week we will look at the predictor-prey model and in particular various Vampire-Human models.

The resources mentioned in Week 10 were:

  • Mathematical Models of Interactions between Species: Peaceful Co-existence of Vampires and Humans Based on the Models Derived from Fiction Literature and Films
    Paper applying predictor-prey model to vampire-human interaction.

  • Lotka-Volterra equations
    Wikipedia article, describing original model.

  • Vampire Apocalypse Calculator

An cool implementation of the vampire-human model with preset values. We will compare our python implementation with this.
